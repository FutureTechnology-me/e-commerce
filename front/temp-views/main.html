How it works

1. You pick the water

Choose from a wide selection of premium water (50+) from all over the world. You can also add water enhancers and tea bags.
We calculate recommended daily water intake for you, to help you decide a quantity.

2. We deliver the water

Your water is delivered to your doorstep, based on delivery schedule you choose: weekly, bi-weekly, monthly or custom.

3. You drink the water

Drink and stay hydrated. We will be sending you a remainder to help you stay at the right level of hydration.
